Stale-While-Revalidate Then Update

I use the service worker to implement the "Stale-While-Revalidate Then Update" strategy on this page.

First of all, the image below loads from the cache then be revalidated. After revalidation, the client gets notified of the new content with a button to replace the image.
After service worker activation, refresh the page and wait until the notification will be appear.
